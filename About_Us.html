About Us
Windsor Diamonds is a business group based in Delhi, with the vision to create something different, Practical & appealing which having complete domestic and international footprint with interests in Jewellery.It is a name well established among the distinguished jewellery entities of India, with a young, dynamic and highly motivated taskforce of professionals. Before 10 years it was founded by Mr.Munish Gupta & Mrs. Monika Gupta with a vision to provide quality kundan, Polki, Diamond jewellery. It all started with the development of a design and making small earrings, rings, and pendants. The inspirations were taken from traditional jewelry. The jewellery made in 22k gold studded with diamond Polki and 14k- 18k gold studded with diamond made according to customers requirement .Our designs are so Unique and Beautiful made through cad & cam also.
